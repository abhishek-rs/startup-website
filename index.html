Not all who wander are lost.

Who are we?

Wanderlost is a company founded by travel-addicts & adventure-lovers with the goal of providing you with the ultimate travel experience. We believe that life should be an adventure and that travelling is the best way to experience freedom. Wanderlost is a travelling service that keeps the final destination a secret from you till the very last moment. We will arrange your flight and stay. But do not worry! You have some say in where you will go. With our app you can make your personal postcard that you think represents your dream holiday destination. From this we determine your preferences and wishes, making sure that you will return with a big smile on your face!

Where can we take you?

Wanderlost specializes in Europe. We know all the wondrous places that no other tourist has discovered yet. All our trips leave from and stay within the Schengen area, so that you (and we) do not have to worry about your visa. The complete list? That is one of our best kept secrets. All we will tell you is that we get you to well-known capitals, isolated beaches and the highest mountains.

How does it work?

The PostCard

The way we determine your travel destination is not random, we will take your preferences into account! But we also know that you do not feel like filling in endless surveys and answering questions. We want to make this fun! That is why we allow you to create a postcard with our app. With the simple drag-and-drop interfaces you can add the elements you would want on the postcard you would send to your friends and family from your ideal location! Do you like the sun? Add it! Or would you rather spend your holiday partying? We have a discoball-sun especially for you! Do not forget to send us a postcard once you are at the destination!
