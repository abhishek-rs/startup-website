About Us

Wanderlost is a company founded by traveler-addicts & adventure-lovers with the goal of providing you with the ultimate travel experience. We believe that life should be an adventure and that travelling is the best way to experience freedom. Wanderlost is a travelling service that keeps the final destination a secret from you till the very last moment. We will arrange your flight and stay. But do not worry! You have some say in where you will go. With our app you can make your personal postcard that you would send from your dream holiday destination. From this we determine your preferences and wishes, making sure that you will return with a big smile on your face!
